Developing the Whole Person

LSTAR Military Academy and College is a leadership institution focused on developing the whole person. The Academy is a college preparatory school for boys, offering middle school and high school. The College is a two-year institution for men and women and has been designated the Military College of Pennsylvania.

Our Mission

To develop individuals to be fully prepared for the responsibilities and challenges of being citizen leaders. We produce graduates who are strong in mind, body and soul.

Our Vision

To be a shining beacon, calling young people from around the world to achieve their full intellectual, physical, moral and leadership potential.

Our Philosophy

To develop the whole person within an integrated academic and military environment. Our tightly structured and supervised cadet-led community helps to inculcate a deep, enduring commitment to responsible and informed citizenship in a democratic society

FAQs

NDA stands for National Defence Academy which is a graduation cum military training college situated in Khadakwasla, Pune. It is one of a kind tri-service academy where cadets of all the three armed forces train together for three years. The intake is twice a year.

NA stands for Naval Academy which is situated in Ezhimala, Kerala. It is also a graduation cum military college exclusively for naval cadets. All the naval cadets in NDA also go to NA after completing their college in NDA.

The age of the aspirant must be between 16.5-19.5 years at the commencement of course, which means to apply for the NDA exam one must be of around 15 years 9 months to 18years 9 months. For example the candidate must have not been born earlier than 02nd January 2003 and not later than 01 January 2006 for NDA I 2022 exam.

The full form of CDS is Combined Defence Services.CDS exam is conducted by UPSC to recruit candidates in Indian Air Force Academy, Indian Military Academy, Indian Naval Academy and Officer’s Training Academy.

For those applying for IMA, INA, and AFA Mathematics is compulsory and those applying for OTA needed not to give Mathematics Exam.

AFCAT stands for Air Force Common Admission Test conducted at National level by the Indian Air Force itself.

(i) Flying Branch through AFCAT and NCC Special Entry: 20 to 24 years on commencement of course, which means to apply this one must be of around 19 years 9 months to 23years 9 months. For example the candidate must have not been born earlier than 02 January 1999 and not later 01 January 2003 for AFCAT I 2022 exam.

NOTE- Upper age limit for candidates holding valid and current Commercial Pilot License issued by DGCA (India) is relax able up to 26 years on commencement of course.

(ii) Ground Duty (Technical &; Non-Technical) Branch: 20 to 26 years as on commencement of course, which means to apply this one must be of around 19 years 9 months to 25years 9 months. For example the candidate must have not been born earlier than 02 January 1997 and not later 01 January 2003 for AFCAT I 2022 exam.
